Fun Facts About Monkeys

  1. Monkeys are omivores
  2. Monkeys have an active IQ equivalent to a 3 year old toddler
  3. Monkeys are capable of learning sign language
  4. Monkeys have an average life span of 20 years in the wild
  5. Monkey's brains are 1/3 the size of human brains
